System Philosophy

Why You Gained 20 kg — The Real Reason

Not laziness. Not willpower failure. Your default environment was broken. The fix is not discipline — it is replacing a bad default with a better one.

Bangladesh Rice

Bangladeshi rice has documented inorganic arsenic contamination (0.2–0.4 mg/kg). Switching to chickpeas, sweet potato, and oats eliminates this exposure.

The Three Laws
Law 1 — Automate Decisions

Every food choice made in real time is a system failure. This system pre-makes every decision. You follow the script.

Law 2 — Protein First, Always

Protein builds muscle, kills hunger (CCK + PYY hormones), and protects muscle during a cut.

Law 3 — Cost Per Gram of Protein

Cheap food that is protein-poor is expensive. Measure cost per 30g of protein — not cost per meal.

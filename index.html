System Philosophy
▼
Why You Gained 20 kg — The Real Reason

Not laziness. Not willpower failure. Your default environment was broken. Dhaka takeout is calorie-dense, protein-poor, oil-heavy, and decision-free. The fix is not discipline — it is replacing a bad default with a better one.

Bangladesh Rice — A Real Reason to Switch

Bangladeshi rice has documented inorganic arsenic contamination (0.2–0.4 mg/kg). Switching to chickpeas, sweet potato, and oats eliminates this exposure while giving better fiber, lower GI, and more micronutrients.

The Three Laws
Law 1 — Automate Decisions

Every food choice made in real time is a system failure. This system pre-makes every decision. You follow the script.

Law 2 — Protein First, Always

Protein builds muscle, kills hunger (CCK + PYY hormones), and protects muscle during a cut. Every meal decision starts: did I hit protein?

Law 3 — Cost Per Gram of Protein

Cheap food that is protein-poor is expensive. Measure cost per 30g of protein — not cost per meal.

Key Protein Notes
Duck Eggs — Your Priority Egg

30% more protein than chicken egg. 3× Vitamin D. Highest DHA of any egg. Find at Karwan Bazar poultry: "হাঁসের ডিম আছে?" — buy whenever available. 4/day target.

Beef — The Micronutrient Profile

Best food source of natural creatine (0.35g/100g). Zinc for testosterone (deficiency = 74% T drop). B12, iron. Ask: "টপসাইড বা সিলভারসাইড লিন কাট — চর্বি কম"

Chickpeas — The Daily Base

GI of 28 = no blood sugar spike. Sprouting multiplies bioavailability — Vitamin C appears from zero, anti-nutrients dissolve. Nightly ritual: 80g dry in C-5 + water = 30 seconds work = 20% of daily nutrition.

Session A — Sunday (Chicken)
8 min active · 35 min total · Mon–Thu supply
  • T+0: Spice water + 4 crushed garlic (wait 5 min!) + kalonji + salt + pepper
  • T+2: Egg-marinate: 2 eggs + ½ tsp blend + mustard oil + chili → coat chicken
  • T+8: Load chicken + 10–12 eggs → passive 12 min
  • T+21: Remove eggs → cold water → passive 15 min
  • T+37: Shred chicken → C-1. Shak in broth 2 min. Strain broth → C-4. Eggs → C-2. FRIDGE within 20 min.
Session B — Friday (Beef)
10 min active · 30 min total · Fri–Mon supply
  • On delivery: Liver in cold salt water soak 20 min. Heart: slice, no soak.
  • T+0: Richer spice water + ginger (mandatory) + garlic + kalonji + marrow bones
  • T+2: Egg-marinate beef: 1 egg + blend + ginger powder + mustard oil
  • T+8: Beef in first → 8 min passive
  • T+17: Add organs + eggs → 12 min passive
  • T+30: Beef → C-1. Organs → C-3. Eggs → C-2. Broth → C-4. FRIDGE 20 min.
Dal Bomb — Optional Wednesday
  • 100g masoor dal + 400ml water + spices + 3 garlic. Boil 18 min.
  • Optional: +50g soya chunks = 26g extra protein
  • Egg Ribbon: Beat 2 eggs. LOW heat. Pour in slow stream, stir = silky ribbons
  • Finish: ½ lemon + ½ tsp mustard oil. 28g base protein / 50g with soya. Cost: ৳25–40.
Why It Doesn't Taste Like Diet Food

Umami (meat proteins + garlic) + Acid (lemon, squeezed after) + Fat finish (mustard oil drizzle) + Heat (chili) + Sweet contrast (banana/dates). All five taste dimensions. Same principle that makes chotpoti addictive.
